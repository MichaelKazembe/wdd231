Site Name

PlantCare â€” This name was chosen to reflect a central, easy-to-remember resource for plant care knowledge. It's beginner-friendly and conveys a community or guide-like feel.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to educate beginners about houseplant care. It will provide information such as plant profiles, care instructions, and a form for users to log their own plant care notes. Dynamic features like modals and data fetching from a plant API will enrich the experience.

Scenarios

  • What type of light and watering schedule does a peace lily need?
  • How do I know if my plant is overwatered?

Color Schema

  • Primary Green (#22663f): Used for headings, buttons, and highlights.
  • Light Background Green (#f5fff5): Used as the main background color to give a calm, natural feel.
  • Accent Yellow (#f2c94c): Used for call-to-action buttons, highlights, and to draw attention to important information.

Typography

  • Montserrat: Used for headings and navigation (clean and modern).
  • Lato: Used for body text for better readability.
