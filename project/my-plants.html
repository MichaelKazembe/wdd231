Practical Plant Care Tips

  • Place your plants in the right light for their species.
  • Water only when the top inch of soil is dry (most houseplants).
  • Ensure good air circulation to prevent mold and pests.
  • Keep plants away from cold drafts and heat sources.
  • Wipe leaves gently to remove dust and allow photosynthesis.
  • Fertilize during the growing season for best results.
